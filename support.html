Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play FINGO?
Simply place 2-5 fingers on your device screen and tap the "Choose" button. FINGO will randomly select one of your fingers!
Can I change the background color?
Yes! Tap the settings icon in the app to choose from different background colors. Your preference will be saved locally on your device.
Does FINGO work offline?
Absolutely! FINGO is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required for any functionality.
Is FINGO safe for children?
Yes, FINGO is completely safe for users of all ages. The app contains no inappropriate content and doesn't collect any personal data.
Why does FINGO need camera access?
FINGO doesn't require camera access. The app only uses touch input to detect finger placement on the screen.
Can I use FINGO on iPad?
Yes! FINGO works on both iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 12.0 or later.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't detecting my fingers properly
Try these solutions:
  • Make sure your fingers are touching the screen firmly
  • Clean your screen to remove any dirt or moisture
  • Try placing your fingers in different positions
  • Restart the app if the issue persists
The app is running slowly
To improve performance:
  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of FINGO
  • Check that your device meets the minimum requirements (iOS 12.0+)
My background color preference isn't saving
Make sure you're tapping the "Save" button after selecting your preferred color. If the issue continues, try restarting the app or reinstalling it.
